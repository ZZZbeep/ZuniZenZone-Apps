🟢 Ventral Vagal — Safe & Social
Your optimal state for healing and growth
This is the state where rewiring actually happens. Your nervous system needs to feel safe before it can build new patterns.
01
Slow exhale (longer than inhale)
02
Hum or sing — activates the vagus nerve
03
Cold water on face — resets the system
04
Name 5 things you can see — grounds you
🟡 Sympathetic — Fight or Flight
High activation · mobilization energy
When you feel anxious, reactive, or overwhelmed. Your system has perceived a threat — real or imagined.
01
Physiological sigh — double inhale, long exhale
02
Move your body — shake, walk, stretch
03
Say aloud: "I am safe. This is not an emergency."
04
Press feet firmly into the ground
🔵 Dorsal Vagal — Freeze & Shutdown
Low energy · disconnection · numbness
When you feel flat, disconnected, unmotivated or numb. This is a protective shutdown response — not weakness.
01
Gentle movement — even just fingers and toes
02
Warm beverage — activates the vagal pathway
03
Sunlight or bright light on your face
04
Speak kindly to yourself — this will pass
